0.0.1 - Convenience Function Added, self tests updated
September 18, 2013
New Feature:
	Two new convenience functions have been added, UltiTestStore and
	UltiTestRestore, that allow for easy saving and restoring of global
	variables. This should help you play nice when having endusers run your
	tests.
Other:
	Additional tests for the plugin itself have been added to
	UltiTestSelfUnit.
